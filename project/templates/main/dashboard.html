Lookup a Book

You can search our database for the books that you want to borrow. If the library has it, you can try to borrow it by submitting a request. Just go to the "Borrow Books" page and fill up the form with any detail you know about the book.

Borrow a Book

You can borrow up to ten (10) books from the library as long as we have it available. All you have to do is click the "Borrow Books" button at the side. There you will be brought to a page where you will find a form which you need to fill up with any details about the book you want to borrow.


Monitor Your Account

This system allows you to monitor your account by going to the "Library Profile" or "Borrow History" pages. In the Library Profile page, you will see all the books you currently have or currently have for approval. In the Borrow History page, you will see the history and status of your requests to borrow books.


Request For A Book!

The school is always open to adding more books to our library to ensure the continuous learning of students. Do you have a book you want to request to be added? Just go to the "Send A Request" page and provide all the details you know about the book you want. This request will be taken into consideration.
